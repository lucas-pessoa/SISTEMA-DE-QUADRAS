Introduction

The platform has the objective of assisting the practice of scheduling the UFSCar sports field. Each schedule can be made by students, teachers and academic organization, and the difference between each is in advance with which they can execute the reserve of the court.

Booking priority

The difference in reservation priority among students, faculty, and academic organizations is in relation to advance reservations.

  • Student:  1 day
  • Academic Organization:  7 days
  • Teacher:  3 days

Availability

The reservation will be made only if the court is available at that time, and within the deadlines of the reservation, the request is executed on a first-come, first-served basis.

Confirmations

To confirm the reservation the user must click the confirm button. If the desired block is available at that time, after confirmation, the action will be processed successfully.

Specifications

In order to execute the reserve of the court it is necessary to inform which sport will be practiced in that environment, being possible the following modalities:

  • Soccer
  • Volleyball
  • Basketball
  • Tennis
